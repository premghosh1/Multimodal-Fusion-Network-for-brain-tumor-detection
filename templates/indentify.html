Cancer Detection Process

ResNet-50 is a 50-layer convolutional neural network (48 convolutional layers, one MaxPool layer, and one average pool layer). Residual neural networks are a type of artificial neural network (ANN) that forms networks by stacking residual blocks. We aim to detect brain tumour using the ResNet-50 CNN architecture trained with our parameters which would help us to achieve our target of 99.3% accuracy.
